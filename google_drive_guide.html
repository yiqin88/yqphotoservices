📂 How to Share Your Google Drive Folder

YQ Photo Services — Step-by-step guide for customers

When placing your order, you'll need to upload your photos to Google Drive and share the folder link with us. Follow these steps to make sure we can access your photos! 📸
1
Open Google Drive
On your phone or computer
Go to drive.google.com or open the Google Drive app on your phone.

Sign in with your Google account if prompted.

Create a new folder specifically for your print order to keep things organised.
2
Upload your photos to the folder
Add all photos you want printed
Open the folder → tap + New (computer) or the + button (phone) → select Upload files → choose all your photos.

Wait for all uploads to finish before proceeding.
⚠️
Make sure all photos are fully uploaded before sharing the link. We can't access photos that are still uploading!
3
Right-click the folder → Share
On computer: right-click. On phone: long-press the folder
On computer: Right-click the folder → select Share
On phone: Long-press the folder → tap the Share icon or ⋮ More → Share
Context menu (right-click)

4
Change access to "Anyone with the link"
This is the most important step!
In the Share dialog, look for General access at the bottom.
Click the dropdown (it may say "Restricted") and change it to "Anyone with the link".

⚠️
Make sure it says "Anyone with the link" — NOT "Restricted". If it stays on Restricted, we won't be able to open your photos!
5
Copy the link & paste it in the app
You're almost done!
Click "Copy link" in the Share dialog.

Go back to the YQ Photo Services order page → Step 4 → paste the link into the Google Drive Folder Link field.

6
Add photo notes (optional but helpful!)
Tell us exactly which photos to print
In the Photo Selection Notes field, you can tell us:

• Which files to print (e.g. "Print IMG_001 to IMG_010 only")
• Any specific editing requests
• Photo orientation preferences
• Which photos go in which size

🎉 That's it!
